Digital Game 1: Click Me!

  • Originality: You need to click the button as fast as you can.
  • Technical Merit: I was able to use the pointer to click a specific object
  • Prototype Postmortem: Using the mouse to click a sprite was a little hard to get working
  • Prototype Assets: I found the click here image on Google: link
  • High Concept: Click the image as many times as you can as it jumps around the screen.
  • Prototype Goal: Esure the game is working and moving around
  • Player Experience Goals: Competitiveness when playing agaisnt friends to score the highest.
  • Gameplay: Players will move the mouse along the screen following a "click here" button. When they click on the image, they score a point.
  • Strategies: Be fast and precise
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You are working hard on your computer when, suddenly, a "click here" button appears, you need to catch this button as many times as possible, or else.....
  • Target Audience: My peers in the class.
  • Play Time: 30 seconds per round
